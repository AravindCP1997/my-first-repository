Business Combination

Elements

Transaction

Business combination is a transaction in which an entity obtains control of a business.

Acquirer

The entity which obtains control of the business acquired is the Acquirer.

Acquiree

The business which is acquired is the acquiree.

Acquisition Date

Date on which the acquirer obtains control of the business

Consideration

Consideratio is the aggregate of assets transferred and liabilities incurred by the acquirer to obtain the control of the business.
